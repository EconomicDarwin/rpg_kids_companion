# Player Journal (kid-facing)

The source for the app's Our Story tab. One entry per played session, authored by
Dad and Claude after each game. This is a rewrite for young readers, never a copy
of the GM session log.

Rules for entries:

- Heading format is exactly `## Session N — Title`. The title is the kid-facing
  title, not the GM log title.
- 2 to 4 short kid-sized sentences (5 is acceptable when the session earned it).
- No em-dashes and no semicolons in the entry text (the heading line is exempt).
- Only table-revealed facts. The export cross-checks that every session log in the
  canon repo has exactly one entry here, and refuses to run otherwise.

## Session 1 — We Are the Lima Sisters

We helped Odbert and saved Roger. Odbert gave us a secret box from Mom. Inside were
magic rings. The rings showed us the truth. We are sisters of the Lima Clan!

## Session 2 — Mom's Secret

Mom is alive and she is very brave. She is leading the Usurper far away from us. The
Usurper's sign is a dark eclipse. We saw it on the cavern wall. Our clan's sign is a
crescent moon and a sunburst.

## Session 3 — The Fire in Rivenshore

Fires broke out in town but we put them out. We kept Bree safe at Moira's sanctuary.
Moira gave us our first spell cards. Rainbow got Crescent Moon Shield and Sky-Saver
got Meteor Strike!
